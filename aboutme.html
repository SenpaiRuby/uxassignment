About SJCC

Discover Japanese culture through our five divisions

Sakuran Japanese Cultural Club

Combining "Sakura" (cherry blossom) from Japan and "Ran" (orchid) from Singapore

We offer five exciting divisions where you can explore Japanese culture through hands-on activities, performances, and creative expression. Join us to learn, create, and connect!

Our Divisions

Origami

Create beautiful paper art from simple to complex designs.

Manga/Anime Illustration

Develop your drawing skills in manga and anime styles.

Japanese Music & Dance

Learn J-pop dances and traditional Japanese performances.

Cosplay

Bring characters to life through costumes and performance.

Film

Explore Japanese cinema and create your own films.

Basic Japanese Lessons

Learn conversational Japanese and cultural context.
