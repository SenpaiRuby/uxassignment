About Sakuran Japanese Cultural Club

Sakuran Japanese Cultural Club (SJCC) combines "Sakura" (cherry blossom) and "Ran" (orchid), representing Japan and Singapore.

Our five divisions bring Japanese culture to life through Origami, Manga/Anime Illustration (MAID), Japanese Music & Dance (JMD), Cosplay, and Film. We host workshops, performances, and cultural exchanges at NYP.

Our Divisions

Origami

Create anything from paper! Beginners welcome with our step-by-step tutorials.

Manga/Anime Illustration (MAID)

Learn drawing techniques from anatomy to backgrounds.

Japanese Music & Dance (JMD)

Become a J-pop idol! Learn various dance styles.

Cosplay Anonymous

Transform into your favorite characters with our guides.

Film Addicts

Learn photography and filmmaking techniques.

Basic Japanese (BJCL)

Monthly conversational Japanese lessons.

Basic Japanese Conversation Lessons

BJCL offers monthly themed sessions to learn practical Japanese phrases with cultural context.
