Membership Terms & Conditions
1. Eligibility
  • Open to all current NYP students with valid student ID
  • Non-students may attend public events as guests
  • Members must maintain good academic standing
2. Membership Responsibilities
  • Attend at least 70% of division meetings
  • Participate actively in club activities and events
  • Respect Japanese cultural practices and traditions
  • Maintain respectful behavior towards all members
3. Code of Conduct
  • No discrimination based on race, religion, or background
  • Maintain appropriate behavior during all club activities
  • Follow NYP's student conduct policies
  • Respect club equipment and facilities
4. Privacy Policy
  • Personal information will be kept confidential
  • Photos/videos may be taken for promotional purposes
  • Members may opt-out of promotional materials
5. Termination
  • Membership may be revoked for misconduct
  • Members may withdraw at any time with written notice
  • Inactive members may have their membership suspended
